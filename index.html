1. Data We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you create an account or use CampusBite, we may collect:

  • Full name
  • Phone number (used for OTP login via Firebase)
  • Email address (for verification and communication)
  • Gender (for personalized recommendations)
  • College or University details

1.2 Usage Data

  • Device information and IP address
  • App usage statistics (such as pages visited, time spent)
  • Crash logs and performance analytics

1.3 Location Data

  • Used to show canteens or vendors available near your campus.
  • Helps schedule pickup timings and order tracking within the campus.

1.4 Payment Information

Payments are securely processed through trusted third-party gateways. CampusBite does not store your card or bank details.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Authenticate users securely through Firebase.
  • Process and manage food orders.
  • Notify vendors about new orders and status updates.
  • Send real-time order updates and pickup alerts.
  • Show nearby canteens and recommended meals.
  • Improve app performance and personalize user experience.

3. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Firebase Authentication and Realtime Database Security
  • Encrypted HTTPS connections
  • Role-based access for users and vendors
  • Regular app updates and data protection checks

4. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • View and edit your personal profile.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Delete your account at any time.
  • Opt out of promotional notifications.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (for authentication, database, and notifications)
  • Google Maps API (for location-based canteen listings)
  • Payment gateways (for secure transactions)

All third-party providers are required to handle your data securely and in compliance with privacy standards.

6. Children's Privacy

CampusBite is intended for university students aged 16 and above. If we discover that we have collected information from users under this age, we will delete it immediately.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be notified through the app and updated on our website.
